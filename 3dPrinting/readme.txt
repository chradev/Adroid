All STL files can be printed without modification except GT2_60T_*.stl which should be scaled down 10 times.
Some details like GT2_60T_*, LongArm_* and MotorMount_* could be printed with fill density down to 25%.
Some details like GT2_60T_*, Hub_* and Pololu90_* could be printed with lower velosity down to 25/10 mm/s
